User Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your data associated with the application E-commerce Automation AI, you can request data deletion at any time.

How to Request Data Deletion

Please send an email to:

support@example.com

Include the following information in your request:

  • Your Instagram username
  • Your Facebook account email (if applicable)
  • A short message requesting deletion of your data

What Happens After Your Request

Once your request is received, we will:

  • Delete stored Instagram access tokens
  • Remove stored Instagram posts and metadata
  • Delete any associated user data from our systems

All deletion requests are processed within 48 hours.

Contact

If you have any questions about your data, please contact:

support@example.com
