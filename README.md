## 2. Information We Collect

When you connect your Instagram account to our application, we may collect the following information through the Instagram Graph API:

* Instagram username
* Instagram account ID
* Public profile information
* Instagram posts and captions

* Comments and engagement data

We only access data that you explicitly authorize when connecting your account.
